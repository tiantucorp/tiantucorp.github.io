TrueVoice Mic: Bone‑Conduction Simulation

Your voice always sounds richer and warmer inside your head than in recordings.
TrueVoice Mic helps you recreate that “inner voice” by enhancing low frequencies and gently shifting pitch to reduce the sharpness of air‑conduction sound.

Step 1) Training Screen:

- Record or Pick an audio file

- Adjust the four filters: Frequency, Body EQ Gain, Warmth Gain, and Pitch Shift

- Tune them until you find the TrueVoice you love

- Save your settings as your personal profile

Step 2) Deployment Screen:

- Record and instantly hear your voice transformed with your TrueVoice profile

- Stream your TrueVoice live to your PCSpeaker

PC Drive

Turn Android phone into PC Drive for Windows, and send files quickly to PC.

Step 1) (Install Android App from Google Play Store,or from Samsung Galaxy Store.) How to connect:
- Scan QR code
- or enter IP and port
- Make sure service of Windows Suite is reachable

Step 2) Install Windows PCSpeaker Suite(2.0 and above) in PC from Microsoft Store. or Install Windows Suite in PC.

In the Android App, after you scan or enter IP, it will get connected. In the Windows, once connected, Windows Explorer will show Android Storage as Z Drive of PC, and be able to manage Android files freely.

Step 3) In the Android Files or Gallery Apps, you may want to Send files to PC, click Share and choose PCDrive, will upload files directly to Download directory of PC.

Warning: if you are only able to Send files to PC, it means that you might have installed Windows PCSpeaker App in PC. Please install Windows PCSpeaker Suite(2.0 and above) in PC.

PC Speaker Premium

Turn Android phone into PC Speaker with premium features, including Smart Replay feature.

Step 1) (Install Android App from Google Play Store,or from Samsung Galaxy Store.) How to connect:
- Scan QR code
- or enter IP and port
- Make sure service of Windows App is reachable

Step 2) Install Windows App from Microsoft Store (1.1 and above) or Install Windows App in PC.

In the Windows App's Dashboard, click on "Capture Tab Audio" Button. In the Android App, click on Speaker->Start Speaker->Connect to Remote Speaker, and you will hear the audio stream from PC Browser Tab. 

Click on "Connect with Smart Replay" Button to try out Smart Replay feature.

Warning: if you get "Cannot GET /premium/", it means that your Windows App is not up to date.
